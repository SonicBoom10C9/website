Sharkord offers a free service that provides subdomains under the `shrkrd.site` domain. This allows anyone to host Sharkord with https even if they don't own a domain of their own. This is especially useful for users who want to quickly set up a server for testing or small communities without the hassle of purchasing and configuring their own domain.

## Claim Your Subdomain

Go to [https://domains.sharkord.com](https://domains.sharkord.com), register and claim your free subdomain. Everyone gets up to 3 subdomains, so you can create different ones for testing, friends, or different communities.

## Subdomain Format

Your subdomain will be in the format `yourname.shrkrd.site`. For example, if you choose `myserver`, your server will be accessible at `myserver.shrkrd.site`.

## How It Works

When you claim a subdomain, you should also point it to your Sharkord server's IP address. This allows users to access your server using the subdomain instead of an IP address, making it easier to remember and share.

After claiming your subdomain, you should wait a few minutes for DNS propagation. Once it's ready, you can access your Sharkord server using the subdomain you claimed.

## No Abuse Policy

- Any subdomain that is found to be used for abuse, spam, or violating our terms of service may be suspended or removed without notice.
- Any other purpose than hosting a Sharkord server. Using them for other services will result in a ban from the service.
- Subdomains that are inactive for more than 7 days may be reclaimed and made available for others to claim.
- We reserve the right to suspend or remove any subdomain at our discretion.
